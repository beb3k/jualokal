# Keep communication and payment in Jualokal

Jualokal keeps item questions, handover coordination, and payment inside the app and prohibits sharing contact details, personal addresses, bank details, or external payment links. Although WhatsApp would be familiar and convenient, moving conversations outside Jualokal would undermine escrow, remove dispute evidence, enable physical browsing and fee avoidance, and expose personal location information before the structured handover flow permits it.

During the prototype, buyers and sellers have no free-form chat. Before purchase, a buyer may send a Structured Question Request, which the seller answers by improving the listing so every buyer sees the same information. After purchase, scheduling and handover coordination use controlled choices and system messages. Required descriptions in Material Mismatch Claims and Safety Reports remain separate controlled forms. This restriction is provisional and should be reassessed after testing.
