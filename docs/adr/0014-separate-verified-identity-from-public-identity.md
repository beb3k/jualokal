# Separate verified identity from public identity

Identity Verification makes a member accountable to Jualokal but does not expose their full legal identity to other members. Public Identity contains only the verified first name and last initial where applicable, or the person's single name for a mononymous identity, plus the Trust Summary. Full legal names, identity numbers or images, verification selfies, and biometric information remain hidden. Profile pictures are optional and do not need to show a face.
