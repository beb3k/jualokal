# Separate membership from selling

Every Verified Member may view listings and buy, but creating a listing requires Seller Activation. The additional step confirms the private Home Anchor needed to enforce the Seller Convenience Zone, establishes a payout destination through the Payment Partner, and records acceptance of Jualokal's listing and handover rules without requiring manual approval. During the prototype, five Demo Sellers provide the public sample inventory; protected builder accounts may also activate selling for private testing.
