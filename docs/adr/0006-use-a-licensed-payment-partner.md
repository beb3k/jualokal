# Use a licensed payment partner

Jualokal does not directly hold or move buyer funds. During the hackathon prototype, payment, Escrow, release, and refund are simulated and no real money enters the system. At launch, a licensed Payment Partner accepts and holds buyer funds and carries out Jualokal's release or refund decision. This preserves Jualokal's authority over transaction completion without making Jualokal the custodian of customer funds.
