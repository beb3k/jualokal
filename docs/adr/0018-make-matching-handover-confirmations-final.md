# Make matching handover confirmations final

The buyer confirms that they inspected and accepted the item against the Purchase Snapshot, and the seller confirms that they handed over that item. When both declarations match, the sale becomes final, simulated Escrow releases immediately, and the handover counts toward Tier Progress. An ordinary Material Mismatch Claim must be raised before confirmation and before the buyer takes the item. Serious suspected fraud or safety misconduct may still be reported afterward, but reporting does not automatically reverse payment. An unmatched Handover Confirmation remains evidence rather than completion; it neither releases nor refunds Escrow automatically. Jualokal retains final authority only over incomplete, inconsistent, or disputed confirmation states.
