# Allow cross-listing before checkout

A seller may advertise an item elsewhere and may deactivate its Jualokal listing until a Checkout Hold begins. The hold temporarily prevents editing or deactivation, and successful payment makes item availability binding. Seller Unavailability after payment produces a full buyer refund, removes the listing, and gives the seller a Reliability Strike whether the item was lost, damaged, sold elsewhere, or withdrawn for a higher offer. This preserves seller flexibility before commitment without transferring inventory risk to the buyer afterward.
